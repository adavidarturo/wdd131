Site Plan Document

1. Site Name

Name: Fuxion Natural Health Catalog

Description: I chose this name because it clearly communicates the focus on Fuxion's natural health products and reinforces the idea of a personal, interactive, and easy-to-use catalog.

Suggested domain: catalogosaludfuxion.com (not yet defined)

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to offer an interactive and visually appealing catalog of Fuxion products. The site will allow customers to:

  • Browse products with clear images and updated prices.
  • Access dropdown menus with objective and concise information.
  • Learn about benefits, main ingredients, and usage instructions.
  • Enjoy a responsive experience on any device.

3. Scenarios

Questions a visitor might ask:

  • What are the main benefits of Fuxion product X?
  • What is the updated price of this supplement?
  • How is this product prepared and consumed correctly?
  • What natural ingredients does it contain and what are they for?
  • Can I see all products organized by category (energy, wellness, weight control, etc.)?

4. Color Schema

  • Sky blue (#1e90ff): Used in headers, buttons, and accents and more
  • White (#ffffff): Used as the main background color.

5. Typography

  • Poppins (sans-serif): For titles and headers, modern and clear.
  • Arial (sans-serif): For paragraphs and body text, readable and standard.
